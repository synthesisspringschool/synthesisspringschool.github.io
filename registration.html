Registration fee

Registration for the Spring School costs 350 Euro. This fee covers the cost of all training, including certification and all programme activities, along with refreshments and some food and drink. The following are included in the fee:


  • All teaching activities
  • Mid-morning/afternoon refreshments (tea/coffee/fresh drinks and small snacks)
  • Welcome mixer with drinks and snacks
  • Final School dinner (including some drinks)
  • Excursion to a local nature reserve including transport and entrance

The fee does NOT include travel to Almería, hotel accommodation, or travel from your hotel to the School each day.

Registration has been kept as low as possible to make the course affordable. Participants from Low- and Middle- Income Countries, Spain and Portugal can apply for financial support with their tuition directly from the University of Almería. This process is handled by the University and whilst we can advise you on the process, we unfortunately are not included in any decisions. If you would like to apply for a bursary, please contact Maria from UAL for more information.




Registration process

In order to register for the Spring School you must apply through the University of Almería's website. You must first pay a deposit of 105 Euro, which is refundable until the 14th February. After this date, you may not receive a refund if you cancel, so please be sure you can attend before beginning your registration. After this date, you will be invited to complete your registration by paying the remaining 245 Euro.

The standard registration form must be filled out through the University of Almería's website below, and unfortunately it is only available in Spanish. We have provided full, step-by-step instructions here. Please read them carefully before beginning your application.
